ABOUT REVIVE

Harald Ott and his researchers have been working in the area of stem cell and genetic development at Harvard Medical to help patients by developing tools to improve the lives of those with end organ failure.

DID YOU KNOW?

End organ failure is the leading health care challenge in the Western world.

Approximately 5.7 million Americans suffer from heart failure.

16 million Americans suffer from chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD).

More than 660,000 Americans suffer from kidney failure.

114,000 people in the US are currently waiting for an organ transplant

ORGAN REGENERATION PROCESS

The organ regeneration, transplantation and bioengineering process from start to finish, can be broken down into 3 main steps.

DECELLULARIZATION

Strip away all living cells from the organ. Organ sits in vessel while chemicals wash away DNA lipids, proteins, sugars and other cellular material.

SCAFFOLDING

Preserve the organâ€™s blueprint while its washed empty. The matrix of collagen and structural proteins that hold everything together will remain.

REPOPULATION

Place organ in a bioreactor chamber that stimulates the environment of the body. Organ is then repopulated with cells that are immunologically matched with patient and after a few days of growth, the tissue becomes functional.
